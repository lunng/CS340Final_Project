About Us


Hello! We are the Corvo databasers. Our goal with this project was to create a comprehensive comparison of past and present NBA players. This database was scraped using beautifulsoup and wikipedia. The data could be cleaner, but we did our best to show a graphical representation of important career statistics for every player that is or was in the NBA. We hope you enjoy!
